Bhakti Tour Uttarakhand

Day 1) Delhi to Haridwar (By Surface 230 Kms 6 hrs )

On arrival in Delhi Railway station you will meet and assist by our representative and drive to Haridwar. On arrival Check in hotel. After fresh up visit to Mansa Devi, Ganga Ji ki Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Overnight stay at Haridwar.

Day 2) Haridwar - Rishikesh – Haridwar

Post breakfast visit to Chandi Devi, Daksh Temple, Jain Temple, Ram Jhula, Bharat Mata Mandir, Swrag Ashram. Overnight stay at Haridwar.

Day 3) Haridwar to Delhi (By Surface 230 Kms 6 hrs )

Post breakfast check out from hotel & proceed to Delhi on arrival visit to Lotus Temple, Drive pass India Gate parliament House, President House. Later Proceed to railway station for dropping.
