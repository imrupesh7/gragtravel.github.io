Blessed Himachal Tour

Day 1) Delhi to Shimla (365 Kms 10 Hrs)

On arrival Delhi Railway station/Airport you will meet and assist by Garg Tours & Travel representative and Proceed to Shimla. En route visit to Pinjore Garden, Timber Trail. On arrival check in hotel. Overnight stay at Shimla.

Day 2) Shimla

Post breakfast visit to Kufri. Vice regal Lodge, Christ Church, The Grind lays Bank, Mall road. Overnight stay at Shimla. .

Day 3) Shimla to Manali (265 Kms 8 Hrs)

Post breakfast checkout from the hotel & proceed to Manali. On arrival check in hotel. Overnight stay at Manali.

Day 4) Manali

Post breakfast visit to Hadimba temple, Manu temple, Vashisht Bath, Van Vihar, Tibetan Monastery, Club House. Overnight stay at Manali

Day 5) Manali

Post breakfast visit to Solang valley, Later back to hotel. Overnight stay at Manali.

OR

Excursion to Rohatang Pass till snow point (Excursion to Rohtang Pass (Guest Own on direct payment & Subject to Permit). Situated at an altitude of 3979 mtrs above sea level and 51 kms outside Manali is the mighty Rohtang Pass - the gateway to Lahaul-Spiti valley. It affords a wide panoramic view of the mountains. Here one sees the majesty of the mountains at its height and splendour. At the top of the pass the air seems to glitter against the snow as you look down over herringboned ridges into the Lahaul valley. Evening at Leisure. O/N Hotel.(Closed on Tuesday)
Note: Rohtang Pass Closed from Dec to May Month due to snow

Day 6) Manali to Dharmshala (280 Kms 10 Hrs)

Post breakfast check out hotel & proceed to Dharamshala. En route visit to Kullu Vaishno Devi Temple, Kullu shawal weaving Ind. Chamunda Devi Temple. On arrival check in hotel. After fresh up visit dal lake. Overnight stay at Dharmshala.

Day 7) Dharamsala - Dalhousie (120km/3.5 hrs)

Post breakfast Post breakfast check out hotel & proceed to Dalhousie. Also visit to Baijnath - War Memorial, Kangra Art Museum, McLeodgnj, St. Johns Church etc. Later depart to Dalhousie. On arrival check in at hotel. Overnight stay at Dalhousie.

Day 8) Dalhousie

Post breakfast Full day sightseeing to Khajjiar, Chamba Valley, Laxminarayan temple and Bhuri Singh Museum, back to hotel. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 9) Dalhousie to Katra (240 KM, 8 hrs)

Post breakfast check out from the hotel & proceed to Katra, On arrival check in hotel. Overnight stay in Katra.

Day 10) Katra

Full day Devi Darshan. Overnight stay at Katra.

Day 11) Katra to Amritsar (320 km 08 hrs)

Early morning check out hotel & proceed to Amritsar. On arrival directly go to wagha border later depart to hotel for check in. Overnight stay in Amritsar.

Day 12) Amritsar to Chandigarh (235 Kms 8 Hrs)

Post breakfast check out hotel & visit to Jallianwala bagh, Golden Temple, Durgiana Temple & proceed to Chandigarh. On arrival check in hotel. Overnight stay at Chandigarh.

Day 13) Chandigarh to Delhi (250 km, 5 hrs)

Post breakfast check out hotel & visit to Rose Garden, Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake. Later depart to Delhi. On arrival drop at Railway station/Airport.
