Rajasthan Package

Day 1) Udaipur

On arrival pick up & you will be greeted by our executive & depart to your previously booked hotel, check in at hotel, Udaipur, 'the city of lakes' is admired for its fairy-tale palaces, lakes, temples, and gardens among many more. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay at Udaipur.

Day 2) Udaipur

After breakfast at hotel. Morning city sightseeing tour. Built in 1559 A.D. by Maharaja Udai Singh, the city has been described as the most romantic spot on the continent of India by Col. James Todd. The places of interest include City Palace which has several carved balconies, arches and decorated pillars, Zenana Mahal, Fateh Prakash, Durbar Hall and Shambhu Niwas. Also visit Jagdish temple, Sahelion-ki-Bari (queen's resort for the friends). Evening Motor launch cruise(Cruise not included) on the placid waters of Lake Pichola. Overnight stay.

Day 3) Udaipur to Mount abu (190 KM, 3 Hrs)

After breakfast at the hotel drive to Mount Abu. Later visit to Dilwara Temples consisting of five Jain Temples built by beginning of the 11th century onwards With intricate ornamental carvings represent the climax in the art of decoration in stone. Visit also the Achalgarh Fort famous for its Shiva Temple. Before returning to your hotel, we suggest you to enjoy a boat-ride (at your own cost) on the placid water of Nakki Lake studded with little islets. Check in hotel, overnight stay to Mount abu

Day 4)Mount abu to Jodhpur (270KM, 5hrs)

After breakfast at the hotel drive to Jodhpur. Afternoon visit the Meharangarh Fort, situated on a low sandstone Hill, including Moti-Mahal and Phool Mahal. Also visit Jaswant Thada, an imposing Marble Memorium built in 1899 and the museum located in the Umaid Public Gardens. Overnight Jodhpur.

Day 5) Jodhpur to Udaipur (250 KM, 4hrs & 30 min)

After breakfast check out from the hotel & depart to Udaipur, on arrival in Udaipur drop at airport

Kindly Find The Travel Tips As Per Bellow

1) Weather Conditions::- WBeing a desert place for the major stretch of the state, the weather in Rajasthan during the day gets hot. Especially in summer i.e. May to September is when the temperatures soar to great heights. To avoid this, plan a trip in a better season.
The ideal time for traveling to Rajasthan is October-March

2) Must carry :- Woolen clothing, Umbrella, Raincoat, Trekking shoes, Walking stick , Gloves, Jacket,

3) Id proofs :-

As per the Government directives it is Mandatory for all the guest (Foreign Nationals , NRI’s and Indian Nationals) to show their Photo ID proof at the time of Check-In which can be in form of ‘Passport and Visa Copy, Driving License, Voter ID Card and Aadhar Card failing which the hotel reserves the right of refusal in allocating a room.

4) Safety Tips:-

  • Do not go out alone during late evenings and night.
  • Make a note of emergency numbers and details of the hotel where you are stay
  • Avoid taking eatables offered by the unknown people.
  • keep your cameras ATM, Cash and other devices safely in a bad and check regularly.
  • Do not trust strangers and follow only Travel Partners TIPS
  • Always ask for authorized tourist guides who have a badge provided by the Indian Government. and your travel Agency.
