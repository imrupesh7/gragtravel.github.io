GOLDEN TRIANGLE WITH BHARATPUR & RANTHAMBORE

Day 1) Delhi

On arrival in Delhi Railway station/Airport you will meet and assist by Garg Tours and travelepresentative and drive to hotel. After fresh up visit to Red Fort, Chandni chowk Market. Overnight stay at Delhi.

Day 2)Delhi to Agra (230 km 6hrs)

Post breakfast check out from hotel & proceed to Agra. En route visit Mathura Vrindavan temples Dwarkadhish Temple, Banke Bihari Mandir, Nidhivan Temple, Shri Krishna Balaram Mandir etc. later drive to Agra, on arrival check in at hotel. Overnight stay at Agra.

Day 3) Agra to Bharatpur (100 km 3 hrs)

Post breakfast check out from the hotel & visit Taj Mahal & Red Fort. Later proceed to Bharatpur. En route visit to Fatehpur sikri. On arrival Bharatpur check in hotel. Overnight stay at Bharatpur.

Day 4) Bharatpur – Ranthambore (210 Kms 05 hrs)

Post breakfast check out from the hotel & visit to Bird Sanctuary for Keoladeo National Park with Rickshaw Ride. Later proceed to Ranthambore. On arrival check in hotel. Overnight stay at Ranthambore.

Day 5) Ranthambore

Enjoy morning and afternoon Jeep ride for National Park. Overnight stay at Ranthambore.

Day 6) Ranthambore – Jaipur (190Kms. 4 hrs)

Post breakfast check out from the hotel & proceed to Jaipur. On arrival check in hotel. Evening leisure. Overnight stay at Jaipur.

Day 7) Jaipur Local Sightseeing

Post breakfast visit to Amber Fort, Jal Mahal. Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal( Palace of winds). Birla Temple, City palace & local markets. Overnight stay at Jaipur.

Day 8) Jaipur to Delhi (250 km 4hrs)

Post breakfast check out from hotel & proceed to Delhi. On arrival in Delhi drop at Railway Station/ Airport.

Kindly Find The Travel Tips As Per Bellow

1) Weather Conditions::- WBeing a desert place for the major stretch of the state, the weather in Rajasthan during the day gets hot. Especially in summer i.e. May to September is when the temperatures soar to great heights. To avoid this, plan a trip in a better season.
The ideal time for traveling to Rajasthan is October-March

2) Must carry :- Woolen clothing, Umbrella, Raincoat, Trekking shoes, Walking stick , Gloves, Jacket,

3) Id proofs :-

As per the Government directives it is Mandatory for all the guest (Foreign Nationals , NRI’s and Indian Nationals) to show their Photo ID proof at the time of Check-In which can be in form of ‘Passport and Visa Copy, Driving License, Voter ID Card and Aadhar Card failing which the hotel reserves the right of refusal in allocating a room.

4) Safety Tips:-

  • Do not go out alone during late evenings and night.
  • Make a note of emergency numbers and details of the hotel where you are stay
  • Avoid taking eatables offered by the unknown people.
  • keep your cameras ATM, Cash and other devices safely in a bad and check regularly.
  • Do not trust strangers and follow only Travel Partners TIPS
  • Always ask for authorized tourist guides who have a badge provided by the Indian Government. and your travel Agency.
